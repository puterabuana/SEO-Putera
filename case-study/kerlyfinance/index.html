01 / Overview

A strong technical base that needed deeper publishing signals.

Kerly Finance is a market-intelligence publisher covering macro, equities, crypto, forex, bonds, commodities, and risk sentiment.

The technical base was already healthy: HTTPS, canonical, robots.txt, sitemap, schema, responsive rendering, and image alt text were present. The largest opportunities were content depth, clearer alignment between headings and page copy, stronger internal and external link context, and a smaller rendered-content gap for AI crawlers.

Evidence disclosure

Four tools have valid before-and-after reports dated 09 and 10 Jul 2026. A fifth post-audit file supplied under a Semrush filename is actually a Yoast report and is labeled correctly here. 02 / What changed

Six focused improvements, without rebuilding the site.

01

H1 aligned to the publishing product

The homepage H1 moved from "Market news that turns noise into tradable context" to "Market intelligence that turns news into financial signals," making the page purpose more explicit.

On-Page · Content SEO
02

Meta description expanded and clarified

The description grew from 142 to 151 characters and now describes market intelligence briefs across stocks, crypto, forex, bonds, and commodities.

Metadata
03

Content depth increased

Seobility counted 593 words before and 1,015 after; SEOptimer independently measured 696 before and 1,140 after. Different crawlers agree on the direction of change.

Content SEO
04

Authority context added

Four relevant external sources - the Federal Reserve, BLS, U.S. Treasury, and SEC - were linked from the editorial experience, moving Seobility from zero to four external links.

E-E-A-T · Links
05

Link structure strengthened

Seobility's link-structure score increased from 65% to 90%, while Rank Math found the internal-to-external link balance remained healthy.

Internal Linking
06

Rendered-content gap reduced

SEOptimer measured rendered content falling from 43% to 33%, improving the amount of important content available in the initial HTML for crawlers and LLMs.

04 / Limitations

What these score changes do not prove.

01

No ranking or traffic claim

These reports measure technical and on-page checks. They do not establish a lift in organic traffic, rankings, subscriptions, or commercial outcomes.

02

Rank Math stayed at 90

Rank Math weighs a different checklist and remained unchanged even while Seobility, SEO Site Checkup, and SEOptimer improved.

03

Yoast disagreed with other crawlers

The post-audit Yoast review reported missing title and canonical signals that the other tools detected. This disagreement is preserved instead of hidden.

04

Backlink authority is still weak

SEOptimer continued to grade links at F and reported no backlink authority. Off-page acquisition was outside this optimisation pass.
