01 / Overview

A coffee commerce experience that started with too little crawler-visible content.

Meridian Roasters is a specialty coffee e-commerce site featuring origin-led products, wholesale access, and purchase flows for fresh roasted beans.

The first crawl found an unusually thin homepage: 114 words, no detected media files, only two internal links, a missing H1, and zero scores for server and link structure. The later run found 524 words, nine media files, 31 internal links, and a complete 14-page crawl.

Evidence disclosure

All eight supplied PDFs were generated on 11 Jul 2026. The RankNow comparison is not a like-for-like page count: coverage expanded from 2 to 14 pages and its issue count rose from 4 to 11. The score gain is retained, but the wider scope and disagreement are visible throughout this page.

04 / Limitations

What the improvement does not prove.

01

No revenue or ranking claim

These reports verify technical and on-page changes. They do not establish more organic traffic, better rankings, conversions, or coffee sales.

02

RankNow coverage changed

The before run crawled two pages while the after run crawled 14. Its score rose, but issues also rose from four to 11, so the result is not a simple like-for-like comparison.

03

Link authority is unresolved

SEOptimer still grades links at F, and Seobility found zero external links. Backlink acquisition was outside this implementation pass.

04

Performance work remains

The after Site Checkup report still flags render-blocking resources, modern image formats, sizing, distorted images, analytics, and email-security items.
