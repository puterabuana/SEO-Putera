01 / Overview

A local restaurant site with a strong concept and uneven audit signals.

Ember Slice is presented as a Williamsburg coal-fired pizza restaurant, with an eight-page experience covering the menu, story, oven, journal, and visit information.

The visual identity was already distinctive. The optimisation pass focused on clearer metadata, checker-level technical failures, image delivery, and crawler-readable signals while preserving the site's editorial personality.

Evidence disclosure

The ten supplied source files cover before and after runs across Rank Math, RankNow, Seobility, SEO Site Checkup, and SEOptimer. A live Google PageSpeed Insights run was captured on 11 Jul 2026 at 16:51 WIB: mobile performance 91 and desktop performance 100. It is after-only evidence. RankNow's headline score remained 98 and several of its sub-scores declined, so that disagreement is shown rather than hidden.

0190→97

02 / What changed

Five focused improvements, with the original design preserved.

01

Metadata tightened

The meta description was shortened from 166 to 154 characters and sharpened around coal-fired pizza and the Williamsburg, Brooklyn location.

Metadata · Local intent
02

Rank Math failures cleared

Three failed checks in the first run were reduced to zero, taking the score from 90 to 97 while keeping one warning visible.

Technical SEO
03

Meta quality reached 100%

Seobility's meta-information score rose from 90% to 100%, while page quality, structure, links, and server checks remained strong.

On-Page SEO
04

Image delivery improved

The modern-image-format warning disappeared from the later Site Checkup report, aligning the live site with WebP delivery. Sizing warnings still remain.

Performance · Images
05

Checker issues reduced

Passed Site Checkup tests increased from 49 to 52 and failed checks fell from nine to seven, while SEOptimer recommendations fell by three.

Validation
06

Scope stayed honest

No off-page authority or traffic result is implied. Remaining link, image sizing, and crawler disagreements are documented for the next pass.

04 / Limitations

What these score changes do not prove.

01

No ranking or order claim

The reports verify checklist changes, not higher search rankings, more visitors, more table bookings, or more pizza orders.

02

RankNow did not improve

Its headline score stayed at 98 and the second run showed three issues instead of two. Its SEO and accessibility sub-scores also moved down.

03

Image sizing still needs work

The later Site Checkup report still flags improperly sized or distorted images. Modern formats improved, but image delivery is not finished.

04

Off-page authority remains thin

SEOptimer continued to grade links at F, and the supplied audits show very limited backlink authority. Link acquisition was outside this pass.
