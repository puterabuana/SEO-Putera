01 / Overview

The assignment was local clarity, not just a higher score.

Wena.my.id is a local business landing page for Wena Self Photo Studio in Tugumulyo, Musi Rawas.

The SEO work focused on making the page easier to understand for both people and crawlers: clear local positioning, title and description fit, search preview quality, social metadata, alt text, crawlability, and practical page performance checks.

Evidence disclosure

The figures on this page come from Rank Math, Seobility, SEO Site Checkup, SEOptimer, and Semrush reports generated on July 5, 2026. An earlier Rank Math run on July 3 scored 92/100; the re-audit two days later scored 88/100, and the more recent figure is used here. Tool scores are directional and do not independently prove bookings or rankings.

04 / Limitations

What these local SEO scores do not prove.

01

No booking-growth claim yet

The reports validate technical and on-page signals. They do not prove a specific increase in bookings, calls, or map-pack visibility.

02

External authority is still weak

Seobility reported external factors at 3%, and SEO Site Checkup recommended building quality backlinks.

03

Performance still has next steps

SEO Site Checkup flagged render-blocking resources, image sizing, and distorted-image concerns as areas to improve.

04

Some audit warnings need judgment

Automated tools can flag analytics, SPF, or duplicate heading text differently from the actual business priority.
