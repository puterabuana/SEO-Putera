01 / Overview

The assignment was technical completeness, not a visual redesign.

PuteraGani.com is an owned editorial website with static article pages, category pages, and more than one hundred public URLs.

The work focused on making every important signal available and consistent in production: canonical targets, initial HTML, page metadata, JSON-LD, sitemaps, robots controls, images, and validation.

04 / Limitations

What these scores do not prove.

01

No ranking-growth claim yet

The reports validate technical signals. They do not prove a specific change in search rankings or organic traffic.

02

Backlink authority remains weak

SEOptimer assigned an F for Links. Technical completeness does not replace off-page authority.

03

Audit tools disagree

Each provider weighs checks differently. The score is meaningful only alongside its source and date.

04

Some recommendations are contextual

Automated checks can flag items that are not equally important for every site, template, or business goal.
