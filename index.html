Verification method

Every score follows the same evidence rules.

01

Audit the live system

Inspect the pages, templates, crawl paths, metadata, schema, and performance constraints that actually ship.

02

Implement by impact

Prioritize repeatable fixes that improve discovery, clarity, and technical consistency across the property.

03

Validate independently

Score the result with dated third-party tools and direct checks instead of an unsupported completion claim.

04

Disclose the limits

Separate technical health from traffic, rankings, backlinks, and outcomes the available evidence cannot prove.
