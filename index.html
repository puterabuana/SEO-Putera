Verification method

Every project follows the same evidence rules.

01

Audit the live system

Inspect the pages, templates, crawl paths, metadata, schema, and performance constraints that actually ship.

02

Implement by impact

Prioritize repeatable fixes that improve discovery, clarity, and technical consistency across the property.

03

Validate independently

Use dated third-party tools and direct checks instead of relying on an unsupported completion claim.

04

Disclose the limits

Separate technical health from traffic, rankings, backlinks, and outcomes that the available evidence cannot prove.
